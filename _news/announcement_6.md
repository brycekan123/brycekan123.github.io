New preprint out — [RaMem: Contextual Reinstatement for Long-Term Agentic Memory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.22844). Addresses context collapse in LLM agent memory by anchoring retrieved memories to their original episodic conditions, achieving 10%+ average F1 gains across multiple LLM backbones.
